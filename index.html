About

Editor first. Motion designer, always learning.

I started as a video editor working across every format — from fast-paced reels to long-form documentary cuts — and I've spent the last stretch pushing into motion graphics, studying brand-level animation to bring that same polish into short-form content.

Every edit is built around pacing, rhythm, and a clear story — whether that's a 15-second reel or a longer narrative piece. I edit primarily in DaVinci Resolve, with motion work built in After Effects.
